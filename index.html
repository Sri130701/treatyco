About Us

TREATY CO is dedicated to sustainable cocoa sourcing and ethical practices. Our mission is to create a better world, one cocoa bean at a time.

Our Cocoa Journey

We source our cocoa from Bonny and Calabar, known for their rich cocoa heritage. We ensure ethical practices with no human rights violations since 1999.

Empowering sustainability through direct partnerships with smallholder families, regenerative silvopasture farming, blockchain-tracked sourcing, solar-powered micro hubs, and real-time AI monitoring.
